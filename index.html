Parallax Forest

Once upon a time, in the Parallax Forest, there lived a group of fairies. The forest was known for its magical creatures, but the fairies were special. They could create illusions that made the forest appear different. One day, a lost traveler stumbled upon the forest and was amazed by its beauty. The fairies created an illusion to help the traveler find his way home. From that day on, the Parallax Forest was known as a place of both wonder and kindness.
